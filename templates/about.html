How this tool works

The Theorizr uses a bag-of-words approach to classifying texts. What this means is that it looks at what you've written and tries to gues which critical theorist is likeliest to have written it. The data it looks at to make a decision is what words you used, and with what frequency. It compares your word usage patterns to those of the critical theorists in the database.

The math behind this classification is a support-vector machine, which is quite complicated. You can read about it on Wikipedia, and it boils down to mapping the features of various texts in such a way that they are divided into clear groups (in this case, different authors). Then it assesses a new text (yours) and sees where on the map it lands.
